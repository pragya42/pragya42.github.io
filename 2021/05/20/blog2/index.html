About the blog

 May 20, 2021

Well here’s a blog post about the blog itself.

My last few attempts..Well no doubt you understand what happened to them :D.

My first blog was actually set up by a friend, who was tired of reading my first drafts and me gushing about every book I’ve read. So thanks Amartya!

The attempt was quite successful until it wasn’t. I was quite weary of large CMS like wordpress. So I set out to build another website from scratch. Which was a bad idea…well not bad but kind of unnecessary. I brushed up quite a lot of skills that I don’t think I’ll need anytime soon. Although I do understand flexbox well now. Then I started learning about Web design, UI/UX design which led to me also designing my own website. I ended up using purple everywhere. This led me to the world of digital illustrations and I’m still very fascinated by it. All my pintrest boards end up being filled with them.

So after 6 months we’re back here back again! Courtesy of another friend. Thank you Priyanshu for all your late night pep talks. I’m using hugo for the blog, for no particular reason. Made it in 10 mins (Taking marketing cues from HUGO). In time I’ll make it more purple (ahem.. PERSONAL). But for now there is a self imposed rule of “One post per modification”. Well we’ll see how it goes :D.
